Climate Awareness

Understanding climate change and how we can help.

What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term changes in temperature, weather patterns, and global climate caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels.

Why It Matters

Rising temperatures cause melting ice caps, rising sea levels, and extreme weather, affecting life on Earth.

How You Can Help

Save energy, use public transport, plant trees, and educate others to make a difference.
