Waste Management

Proper disposal and recycling for a cleaner earth.

Why Waste Management Matters

Improper waste disposal can harm the environment and our health. Managing waste properly reduces pollution and conserves natural resources.

3Rs: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

We can reduce waste by using fewer resources, reuse items, and recycle materials like paper, plastic, and glass.

Eco-Friendly Tips

Use cloth bags, compost food waste, and avoid single-use plastics to reduce your waste footprint.
