Green Energy

Powering the planet with renewable sources.

What Is Green Energy?

Green energy comes from natural sources like sunlight, wind, rain, and geothermal heat. It's clean, renewable, and reduces our reliance on fossil fuels.

Types of Green Energy

  • Solar Power
  • Wind Energy
  • Hydropower
  • Geothermal Energy
  • Biomass Energy

Benefits

It helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions, lowers air pollution, and creates sustainable jobs.
