Plastic Conscience

Being aware of how plastic affects our environment.

The Problem with Plastic

Plastic pollution harms marine life, clogs rivers, and never truly goes away. It breaks down into microplastics that pollute our food and water.

What You Can Do

Say no to plastic straws and bags, carry a reusable bottle, and support products made from sustainable materials.

Spread Awareness

Educate your family and friends about the importance of reducing plastic use and supporting alternatives.
