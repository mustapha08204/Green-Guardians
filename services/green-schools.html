Green Schools

Building sustainable environments for the future generation.

What Are Green Schools?

Green schools are educational institutions that focus on sustainability and eco-friendly practices. They reduce environmental impact and inspire students to care for the planet.

Key Features

  • Use of renewable energy (solar panels, etc.)
  • Recycling and waste management programs
  • Eco-friendly buildings with natural lighting and ventilation
  • Gardens and green spaces for learning and food
  • Environmental education and student involvement

Why They Matter

Green schools promote healthier learning environments, reduce carbon footprints, and foster a lifelong appreciation for nature and sustainability.
